Who we are
PETROJET CONTRACTING & SERVICE

Petrojet Contracting & Services is a renowned and trusted industry leader in India. Since its establishment in 2020, Petrojet Company has successfully undertaken projects in virtually every sector of the country’s economy, and has played a key role in the overall growth and development of India’s commercial and industrial infrastructure.

Petrojet brings to the table trustable industry experience, and local know how. We have a proven track record of expertise in the Energy and Civil Engineering industry and a diverse portfolio that consists of industrial structures, infrastructure development, pipelines and road construction, as well as luxury villas, office blocks, warehouses and other commercial buildings. Our offshore works portfolio includes bridges, jetties and quay wall construction & dredging, land reclamation and shore protection works.

Petrojet Contracting offers comprehensive solutions to clients by utilizing a skilled team of engineers, technicians, and workers who employ the latest construction methods and machinery. Their turnkey construction services are supplemented by additional support offerings such as landscaping, asphalting, plumbing, and heavy equipment hiring. With a rich history of collaborating with major international contracting companies, Petrojet has the capability to undertake high-value turnkey projects.

Director's Message

The core values of honesty, integrity, excellence, transparency, and customer orientation, which were instilled in the Petrojet corporate culture over years ago, remain the moral compass that guides every facet of the Petrojet Contracting today. We are unwavering in our commitment to these values as we strive towards achieving our vision to become a major regional player in the Energy, Construction and industrial sector and the preferred business partner of choice.

... Through the consistent, timely delivery of quality products and service excellence, Petrojet Contracting has maintained its prominent position in India and Kingdom of Saudi Arabia while strengthening and expanding its network of value-added business partnerships throughout the GCC.
