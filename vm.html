About Us

PETROJET CONTRACTING & SERVICE

Petrojet Contracting & Services Pvt. Ltd. (PETROJET) is an Indian Closed Joint Stock Company established in 2020, licensed by the Ministry of Commerce and Industry. The creation of Petrojet Contracting & Services is a result of the consolidation of Petrojet Group’s core construction and industrial related businesses under one roof.

Since its inception, the Company has emerged as a leading, and integrated Construction Solutions Provider, with operations in India, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Comprised of ten divisions Petrojet Contracting & Services has the vision to become a major regional player providing innovative, turnkey Energy, Construction and Industrial solutions.

The Company aims to provide clients with a convenient and trusted source for a myriad of energy & construction disciplines.
